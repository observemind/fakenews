The Process

How SatyaCheck Works

Three-step pipeline powered by NLP and Machine Learning

01

Text Input

Paste any news headline, article excerpt, or WhatsApp forward. SatyaCheck accepts text in English and transliterated Hindi.

02

NLP Processing

The text undergoes tokenisation, stopword removal, and stemming. TF-IDF transforms it into a 15,000-feature vector space.

03

AI Verdict

Logistic Regression with saga solver classifies the article as FAKE or REAL with confidence score and signal analysis.
